Custom Logos That Speak Your Identity

At C H A R I, I believe a great logo is more than just a symbol — it's the visual cornerstone of your brand. A logo should speak for your business before a word is read or a product is seen. That's why I create logos that are not only visually striking but also conceptually strong, memorable, and versatile across all platforms. Each design is rooted in strategy, ensuring it reflects your values, market, and message.

My logo design services begin with brand discovery — I take the time to understand your business, target audience, and the story behind your brand. Based on this foundation, I brainstorm and sketch multiple concepts that reflect your identity. I then develop professional logo variations, including icon-based, text-based, and hybrid designs, ensuring there’s a solution that fits your needs and style. All logos are created with scalability, color psychology, and usage flexibility in mind.

Whether you're a startup in need of a bold first impression or an established brand looking for a refreshed identity, I tailor every logo to your specific goals. My design process is highly collaborative — you’ll be involved at each milestone with multiple revision rounds to ensure you’re happy with the direction and final result. I can also help define your brand color palette, typography suggestions, and visual tone for broader consistency.

Logo projects are typically completed within 5 to 7 business days. More complex branding packages, which may include icons, brand guides, and secondary marks, may take 7 to 12 business days depending on the project scope. Timelines are always clearly outlined in advance, and I offer rush options if you need it faster. You’ll receive regular previews and have opportunities to provide feedback throughout the design phase.

Upon completion, I deliver a full logo kit that includes your logo in all essential formats — PNG, SVG, PDF, and vector files for print and web. You’ll also get a style guide (optional) with guidelines for using your logo consistently across social media, packaging, websites, and more. My goal is to equip you with a professional brand asset that builds recognition and sets you apart from the competition.
